Farmer Functionalities

AGRICORE empowers farmers by providing easy access to essential tools for improving farming efficiency and profitability. Below are some key features available to you:

  • Get Crop Information: Access detailed information about various crops, including their growing conditions, required nutrients, and more.
  • View Crop Prices: Stay updated with the latest market prices for different crops, helping you make informed decisions about when and what to sell.
  • Location Insights: Understand the best crops for your location based on local climate and soil conditions.
